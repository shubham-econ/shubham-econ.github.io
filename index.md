## About Me

Hello, I am Shubham Bhandare. I am currently working as a Research Assistant at Centre for Policy Research, where I collaborate with Rahul Verma on projects related to political parties, elections, and public opinion.

I completed my Master's degree in Economics from Azim Premji University. My research interests lie at the intersection of political economy, political representation, caste politics, electoral behavior, and public policy.
