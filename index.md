## About Me

Hello, I am Shubham Bhandare. I am currently working as a Research Assistant at the Centre for Policy Research, where I collaborate with Rahul Verma on projects related to political parties, elections, and public opinion.

I completed my Master's degree in Economics from Azim Premji University. My research interests lie at the intersection of political economy, political representation, caste politics, electoral behavior, and public policy.

My current work focuses on historical electoral data, caste representation, founding political parties, and the political determinants of public goods provision. I am also interested in quantitative methods, applied microeconomics, and computational social science.
